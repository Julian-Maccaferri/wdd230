Overview

 

Purpose

 

The overall goal of the Rosario Chamber of Commerce site is to promote the business interests of the members of the organization.

Promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people.

 

Audience

 

Local Business Owners: Age: 25-60.
Occupation: Entrepreneurs, small business owners, and corporate executives.
Interests: Networking, business growth, local economic development.
Needs: Information on membership benefits, upcoming events, networking opportunities, and resources for business development.
